# 4. Design System

A consistent and thoughtful design system is crucial for a good user experience. This document outlines the colors, typography, and components that form the visual identity of QuizzyAI.

## Color Palette

Our color palette is designed to be modern, accessible, and conducive to a learning environment.

### Primary Colors
*   **Primary Blue:** `#007AFF` - Used for primary actions, links, and active states.
*   **Dark Blue:** `#0A2540` - Used for text and backgrounds.

### Secondary Colors
*   **Accent Green:** `#34C759` - Used for success states, positive feedback, and highlighting achievements.
*   **Accent Orange:** `#FF9500` - Used for warnings, attention-grabbing elements, and gamification features.
*   **Accent Red:** `#FF3B30` - Used for error states and critical actions.

### Neutral Colors
*   **White:** `#FFFFFF`
*   **Light Gray:** `#F2F2F7` - Used for backgrounds and dividers.
*   **Medium Gray:** `#E5E5EA`
*   **Dark Gray:** `#8E8E93` - Used for secondary text.
*   **Black:** `#000000`

## Typography

We use a clean and readable font family to ensure that content is easy to consume.

*   **Primary Font:** `Inter` (or a similar sans-serif font like `Roboto` or `San Francisco`).
    *   **Headings:** Bold, various sizes.
    *   **Body Text:** Regular, 16px.
    *   **Captions & Secondary Text:** Regular or Medium, 12-14px.

## Component Library

Our UI is built from a library of reusable React components. This ensures consistency and speeds up development.

*   **Buttons:** Primary, secondary, and tertiary styles.
*   **Cards:** Used for courses, books, and study groups.
*   **Modals:** For certificate viewing, payments, and other pop-up information.
*   **Spinners & Skeletons:** For loading states.
*   **Form Elements:** Inputs, text areas, and selectors with consistent styling and validation.
*   **Icons:** A consistent set of icons for navigation and actions.
*   **Header & Footer:** Consistent navigation and branding across the app.
